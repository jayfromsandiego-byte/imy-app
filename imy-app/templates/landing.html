Questions

Questions, answered.

Is it really free?+
Yes. A complete tribute page stays online forever, free, with no credit card to begin. Plus adds every photo and video, their voice, and living pictures whenever you are ready.
Will it really be kept forever?+
That is our promise. Every tribute is backed up and funded to stay online for generations, so their page is always there when you need it.
What happens if I stop paying for Plus?+
The page stays. The premium features simply rest until you return. We will never take a memory offline or charge a family to keep one alive.
Can I give Plus to a family, as a gift?+
Yes — it is given as often as it is bought. On their tribute page, choose “Give this page”: $97, once, no account needed. The wall opens for the whole family, every waiting memory comes home, and if you wish, a quiet line remembers your gift.
Can family and friends add their own photos and memories?+
Yes. Share one link and everyone who loved them can add photos, videos, and messages. The page grows together over time.
Who can see the page, and is my information private?+
You decide who sees it · public, unlisted, or protected with a password, on every plan, free included. Your photos and words stay private and always yours to export. We never sell your data.
Can I get a printed keepsake book?+
Yes. A printed linen keepsake book and a framed portrait come with Concierge, and can be added to any page.
How do I begin?+
Add their name and a favorite photo, and their page exists from that moment. It takes only a few quiet minutes.
