Questions

Questions, answered.

What does it cost to begin?+
You can create a full tribute page and keep it online without paying. Plus and Heirloom add more when you are ready.
How long does their page stay online?+
As long as you need. We will never charge you to keep a memory online.
Is it private?+
You choose. A page can be kept among family or opened to everyone who loved them. Your photos always stay yours.
Can family and friends add to it?+
Yes. Share one link and everyone can light a candle, leave a rose, and add their own photos and memories. It grows as a family.
Can I start free and upgrade later?+
Yes. Everything you add carries over the moment you upgrade. Nothing is ever locked away from you.
What happens if I stop paying Plus?+
Their page stays online. The premium features simply rest until you return. We never take a memory down.
