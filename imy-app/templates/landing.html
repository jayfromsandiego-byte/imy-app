Is it really free? What's the catch?

It is really free, and it stays online for good. A free tribute carries a small "made with love" credit, holds up to 30 photographs, and has three backgrounds to choose from. We never charge a family to keep a memory online.

Why a one-time price instead of a subscription?

Because a memorial is not something you should pay rent on. Plus and Heirloom are a single gift, once. No recurring bill, no renewal hanging over your family. If you ever choose a monthly option, lapsing it never takes the page down.

What does "kept forever" actually mean?

Their tribute stays online for as long as we operate, backed by a dedicated reserve fund and an independent backup archive, and your photos and words are always yours to export. If we ever had to close, we would give at least 90 days' notice and a complete copy of everything.

Can other people add to it?

Yes. Anyone with the link can light a candle, send a rose, and leave a memory. You review memories before they appear, so their page stays gentle and theirs.

What is the keepsake book?

For Heirloom, a printed book of their tribute, mailed to you to hold and pass down. It can also be ordered on its own at any time.

Prices in US dollars. Plus and Heirloom are one-time payments. Add-ons (a printed keepsake book, extra video, anniversary care) are available separately. Donations made "in their name" go to a charity you choose.
