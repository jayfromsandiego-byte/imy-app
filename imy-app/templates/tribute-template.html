Who she really was

With everything set aside, this was her.

Her church
A hike that ends at a view. She said more to God up there than in any pew.
Her secret kindness
Knitted tiny hats for the children's hospital every winter. Told no one for twenty years.
With her people
Tuesday tea with the girls. Gossip strictly forbidden. Gossip always happening.
Winding down
The crossword, the radio low, feet on the ottoman. Do not call during Jeopardy.

Every stray · dog, cat, or teenager. The door was never actually locked.
